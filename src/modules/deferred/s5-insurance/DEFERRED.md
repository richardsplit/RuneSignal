This module has been deferred from active development and all user-facing surfaces.

**Reason:** Requires licensed actuaries, insurance regulatory approval, and historical claims data before any commercial deployment. Deploying insurance pricing logic without actuarial licensure creates legal liability.

- Actuarial licensure obtained
- Insurance regulatory approval in target jurisdictions
- Historical claims dataset available for model validation
